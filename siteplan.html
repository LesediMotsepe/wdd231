Site Name

Emmarentia Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Businesses in the city of Emmarentia will be able to access resources from this website. In addition to hosting networking events and business directories, the website will promote local shopping and offer information on upcoming events. It seeks to encourage a feeling of community among the businesses, support local companies, and advance local economic growth.

Target Market

Customers and business owners in Emmarentia and the surrounding areas

Site Goals

  • By offering beneficial resources and networking opportunities, you can increase member engagement
  • Encourage community economic development and draw in new companies
  • Boost the chamber's prominence and standing as a reliable resource for local business issues

User Personas

  • Small Business Owner: Maria, a 35-year-old businesswoman, is the owner of a small local boutique. In order to expand her business, she is seeking marketing assistance, business resources, and networking opportunities.
  • Corporate Executive: Diego, a 47-year-old executive, is searching for opportunities for advancement at a major corporation. Partnership opportunities, resources for economic development, and networking with other local business leaders are of interest to his company.
  • The New Resident: Francisco recently moved into the area and is interested in finding out more about the services that are offered. He wants to locate community service opportunities, event calendars, and business directories.

Scenarios

  • A local business owner wants to connect with other business owners by joining the chamber of commerce. They go to the website to learn about the costs, advantages, and application process for membership. A member of the community is searching for the chamber's next workshops and events.
  • They go to the website to sign up for engaging events and peruse the events calendar. An out-of-town visitor is interested in learning more about the local business climate because they are thinking about moving to the area.
  • They look through the chamber's website to learn about local businesses, skilled labor availability, and quality of life.
